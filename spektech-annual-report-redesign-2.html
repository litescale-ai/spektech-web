Frequently asked

Frequently asked questions.

What is SpekTech?
SpekTech connects online consumers, businesses, and funders to land restoration and climate impact projects, including their associated carbon offsets, primarily in semi-arid regions of Africa.
How does SpekTech work?
SpekTech Raise generates funding through eCommerce integrations and subscription management. SpekTrack follows fund distribution in real time. SpekTech Monitor adds analytics, KPI tracking, automated reports, and blockchain-verified records.
Who is SpekTech designed for?
Project managers use it to streamline operations. Donors use it to see where funds go and what impact they make. Stakeholders use the reporting and blockchain-verified data for transparency and accountability.
Where is SpekTech based?
SpekTech is headquartered in Johannesburg, South Africa. It was founded by Ephraim Moss, a software architect, and Michalya Schonwald Moss, a business development and ESG impact specialist.
